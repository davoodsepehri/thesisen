Abstract of Msc thesis
Fuzzy Control of Debutanizer Process in Gas Refineries

The control of debutanizer process in gas refineries is very important due to its influence to efficiency, rate and production capacity

Presently, debutanizer processes are controlling by classic controllers and artificial neural networks are used to improvement in some cases.

In this research, after modeling of a debutanizer process by MATLAB® software, during review the existing common controllers, we will design a PID controller for screening the existing common controller limitations. Then as a solution to obviate or decrease those limitations, we will introduce the fuzzy control system as a better control method with a more satisfactory for debutanizer process control.

In the following the research, we will do the control of a typical debutanizer process with fuzzy control method for control the concentration of BUTAN via regulation the effective manipulated variables of debutanizer column by MATLAB and then, we will compare it with actual classic methods. By result of that comparison, limitations of fuzzy control system will be explained at the end.

After run of this plan, it is expected that the system response is faster without a considerable overshoot. These two benefits on the one hand cause to save the used energy for production and increases efficiency of the production by prevention of used energy loss along dead time and oscillation. And from other hand, by rejection the useless time period including overshoot and undesired responses, it will cause more production compared with old methods.

Keywords: Control of debutanizer; Control of refinery; Oil and gas system control; Industrial Control; Control system; Fuzzy Control; Control
